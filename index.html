Common Questions

Do I need a Google account?

No. You can start scanning immediately. We do not require any accounts or personal information.

Does it save to Google Drive?

No. The app is disconnected from all cloud services to ensure your documents stay 100% private on your device.

How much does it cost?

New users get 3 free trials. After that, the Pro plan is a flat 0.50 GHS for unlimited lifetime access or per month depending on your region.

Can I edit existing PDFs?

Yes, you can import any PDF and use our editor. Each import uses one free trial for non pro users.

Can I scan ID cards and passports?

Yes! Use the dedicated ID Card mode to scan front and back separately the app combines them into one PDF automatically.

What are Smart Spreadsheets?

A built in data grid tool with Excel style formulas, multi currency formatting, chart generation, and local PDF export all from your phone.
